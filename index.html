welcome to hair day salon

In the heart of the city, there’s a salon where your look truly comes to life… but beware: here, creativity has a few… unusual shades.

Among our hair artists, you’ll find a colorist with a touch so precise it seems almost surgical. Some say that when they start mixing colors, the air fills with a rather… peculiar atmosphere. Clients leave with flawless hues, of course, but it’s not uncommon for them to experience slight shivers down their spine or strange dreams about “sharp scissors” the following night. Don’t worry—it’s just the price you pay for gourmet-level beauty!

Then there’s the master of nighttime hairstyles: the hairdresser who transforms your hair into true works of art… of nightmare. Their cuts have edges so sharp you might wonder if those are just scissors or claws. Some clients swear they’ve heard a faint whisper while they worked, but maybe it was just the wind through the hair… or maybe not. If you love to dare with your look, get ready to leave the salon with a hairstyle you won’t easily forget — and perhaps neither will your dreams.

Of course, the rest of our team consists of less… “singular” professionals, but equally skilled, ready to give you elegant cuts and stylish looks.

The salon is open Monday through Saturday, and appointments are highly recommended — for those brave enough to trust truly expert hands.

A perfect cut... or wickedly perfect?

Want to refresh your look or need a hairstyle to die for on a special occasion? Here, every haircut is a little beauty ritual, performed by expert hands... and scissors that seem to have a mind of their own. Our team isn’t made of just hairdressers — think of them as style conjurers. They’ll listen to you, advise you, and then, with a smile (sometimes a slightly unsettling one), they’ll transform your hair into something unforgettable… even in your dreams. For every big event — weddings, graduations, exclusive parties, or mysterious nighttime appearances — we’ve got the right style. Elegant, classic, or with an unusual twist: just ask (but choose your words wisely… the scissors are sensitive). Book your appointment… and get ready to stand out. Beauty, in our salon, always comes with a sharp edge.
